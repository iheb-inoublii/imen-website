Why Green? An Investigation 🔍

We asked Imen why she loves green things. She pointed at a tree. Case closed.

🌳

Trees

Tall, calm, and quietly photosynthesizing — trees are basically nature's introverts, and Imen respects that energy deeply.

🌱

Anything That Sprouts

A tiny leaf appears on a plant and Imen celebrates like it graduated. (To be fair, she knows a lot about graduating this year.)

🏞️

Green Hills

Cap Bon in spring: orange trees, green fields, the sea behind. Imen's screensaver, except it's real and it smells like orange blossom.

🌸

Flowers, All of Them

Jasmine on summer nights, blue flowers for the soul, wildflowers on the roadside. Imen has never met a flower she didn't immediately befriend.

🪴

House Plants

Each one has a name, a personality, and a better care schedule than most humans. They are thriving. They know they're lucky.

🍵

Mint (Honorary Flower)

Green AND it goes in tea? Mint is the overachiever of the plant world. Imen and mint: an alliance older than time.

A Perfect Nature Day, by Imen 🌍

Morning — The walk

A 10-minute walk that becomes 2 hours, because every flower deserves a personal greeting and at least 12 photos.

Noon — The picnic

Under a tree, obviously. Sandwich with harissa, obviously. The ants keep a safe distance. They've heard about the spice.

Golden hour — The photos

847 photos of the same olive tree from slightly different angles. All of them are necessary. Do not question the archive.

Evening — Back home

Waters the plants, tells them about her day, then trades the green world for the screen world. The series won't watch itself.
