Imen's Field Guide to Blue Flowers

Real flowers, real facts, mildly unreal commentary.

🪻

Forget-Me-Not

Tiny, sky-blue, and impossible to forget — basically the flower version of a good series finale.

🌼

Cornflower

So blue that painters used to fight over it. Imen would have won that fight. Politely. With a smile.

🔔

Bluebell

Carpets entire forests in blue every spring. Nature clearly knows Imen's aesthetic and is showing off.

🌀

Morning Glory

Blooms at sunrise and closes by afternoon — the only thing on Earth with a better sleep schedule than Imen during a new season drop.

💧

Hydrangea

Turns blue when the soil is acidic. Imen has been quietly pouring lemon juice in gardens for years. Allegedly.

⭐

Borage

A blue star-shaped flower you can actually eat. Tunisians put it in salads; Imen puts it on a pedestal.

"In a field of roses, she is a forget-me-not. Blue, obviously. Did you even have to ask?"

Nature's To-Do List 🌍
